POUNDED YAM AND EGUSI SOUP

Pounded yam is highly nurishing

Pounded Yam is a very delishious food, It is an African delicacy mostly eaten by the Yoruba people of Nigeria

It is mostly eaten on special ocassions because of the stress involved in preparing it

RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

POUNDED YAM EGUSI SOUP
A fresh tuber of yam Three cups of egusi melon
motal and pestle Pepper
water 250ml of palm-Oil
Stock Fish
Seasoning

PROCEEDURE

POUNDED YAM

  1. Peel the yam
  2. Pash it in a clean water
  3. Boil it on a gas cooker

EGUSI SOUP

  • Blend the egusi mixed with pepper and onions for spice
  • Pour oil into pot on a gas cooker, and allow it to get hot
  • Add the blended Egusi to it, allow it to boil for 10min
  • Then add seasoning and beef or fish
